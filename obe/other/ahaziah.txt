====== Ahaziah ======

===== Facts: =====

Ahaziah was the name of two kings: one ruled over the kingdom of Israel, the other over the kingdom of Judah.

  * Judah's King Ahaziah, son of King Jehoram, reigned for one year (842 BC). Then he was killed by Jehu. His son Joash eventually took his place.
  * Israel's King Ahaziah, son of Ahab, reigned for two years (850-49 BC). He died from injuries suffered in a fall at his palace and was succeeded by his brother Jeroboam.
